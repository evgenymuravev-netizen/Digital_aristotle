FIRST ABU DHABI BANK (FAB)
KEY FACTS STATEMENT — CREDIT CARDS (CONSOLIDATED)
Effective date: 01 May 2026   |   Currency: AED   |   Issued under CBUAE Consumer Protection Regulation

This Key Facts Statement summarises the main features, fees and charges of FAB credit
cards. It is a summary only; please read the full Cardholder Terms and Conditions. All
fees are exclusive of VAT unless stated. Fees and rates are subject to change with prior
notice in line with CBUAE Standards.

====================================================================================
Product Name: FAB Cashback Card
Card Network: Visa

Eligibility
  Minimum monthly income: AED 5,000
  Minimum age: 21 years

Profit / Interest
  Monthly interest rate: 3.25% per month
  Annual Percentage Rate (APR): 39.5% per annum
  Interest is calculated on a daily basis on the outstanding balance.

Fees and Charges
  Annual membership fee (primary card): AED 300
  Supplementary card fee: AED 150
  Foreign currency transaction fee (non-AED markup): 2.99%
  Cash advance fee: 3% of the amount or AED 99, whichever is higher
  Late payment fee: AED 230
  Over-limit fee: AED 273.50
  Minimum amount due: 5% of the outstanding balance or AED 100, whichever is higher

Cashback Rewards
  Earn 5% cashback on supermarket and grocery spends (up to AED 200 per month).
  Earn 3% cashback on dining and restaurants (up to AED 200 per month).
  Earn 3% cashback on school fees and education (up to AED 200 per month).
  Earn 1% cashback on non-AED (international) spends.
  Earn 0.15% cashback on all other retail spends.
  Minimum monthly spend of AED 3,000 is required to earn cashback.
  Total maximum cashback is AED 1,000 per month across all categories.

Benefits
  Complimentary access to 0% instalment plans on eligible purchases.
