McQuaig-style Mental Agility Test

A timed measure of mental agility — how quickly and accurately you reason across verbal, numerical and logical problems. Pick any of the ten forms below. Each is a fresh set of questions with its own 15-minute clock.

How it works

  • 15 minutes per form. The clock starts when you begin and the form submits automatically when it runs out.
  • Work fast but accurately. Every question is worth one mark; there is no penalty for guessing, so never leave a blank.
  • Jump around freely. Use the number palette to revisit or flag questions before time is up.
  • Keyboard friendly: press 1–5 to answer, ←/→ to move, F to flag.

Original practice items written in the spirit of the McQuaig Mental Agility Test. Not affiliated with, or endorsed by, The McQuaig Institute®. For self-practice only — not a clinical or hiring instrument.
